# Business Questions

## 1. Customer & Branch Analysis

### Main Question

How are customers distributed across branches, regions, industries, age groups, gender groups, and customer types?

### Supporting Questions

- Which branches have the highest number of customers?
- Which regions have the largest customer base?
- What is the distribution between individual and corporate customers?
- Which industries contribute the largest customer groups?
- How are customers distributed by age group and gender?

## 2. CASA Transaction Analysis

### Main Question

How do CASA inflow, outflow, net flow, and interbank transactions change over time?

### Supporting Questions

- What is the total number of payment transactions?
- How much money flows into and out of current accounts?
- Which months have positive or negative net flow?
- What is the ratio of interbank transactions?
- Which accounts have the highest transaction value?

## 3. Savings & Mobilization Plan Analysis

### Main Question

How does actual savings mobilization compare with the business plan?

### Supporting Questions

- What is the total savings amount?
- What is the average savings interest rate?
- Which branches achieved or exceeded the mobilization plan?
- Which deposit terms are most common?
- How much savings remain active or have been settled?

## 4. Credit & Repayment Analysis

### Main Question

How much has the bank disbursed, how much principal remains outstanding, and which loans show overdue signs?

### Supporting Questions

- What is the total disbursement amount?
- How much principal has been repaid?
- How much interest has been collected?
- What is the outstanding principal?
- Which loans are potentially overdue?

## 5. Collateral & Risk Exposure Analysis

### Main Question

How well does collateral value cover outstanding loan exposure?

### Supporting Questions

- What is the total collateral value?
- What is the collateral coverage ratio?
- Which branches have high collateral value?
- Which contracts are under-collateralized?
- What is the risk exposure from loans with low collateral coverage?
